# About STEM Lab FPGA

Welcome to STEM Lab FPGA! We are passionate engineering lecturers who are dedicated to promoting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education, especially in the field of engineering.

## About FPGA and DE10-lite

FPGA (Field-Programmable Gate Array) is a versatile integrated circuit that can be programmed to perform various tasks. The DE10-lite board is a development platform that features an FPGA, allowing users to design and implement custom digital circuits.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and engage the young generation, particularly school children, in the exciting world of STEM. We believe that early exposure to STEM concepts can ignite curiosity and interest, paving the way for future engineers and innovators.
