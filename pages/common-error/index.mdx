## Alstar USB Blaster not detectd in Programmer

1. If you ever encounter situation below:

![Intel® Quartus® Prime Lite Edition Design Software](/get-started/no-hardware.png)
> No Hardware found

2. Go to Device Manager > Universal Serial Bus controllers > USB-Blaster 

![Intel® Quartus® Prime Lite Edition Design Software](/get-started/device-manager.jpg)

3. Right click on **USB-Blaster** and select Update Driver

![Intel® Quartus® Prime Lite Edition Design Software](/get-started/update-device.jpg)

4. Select **Browse my computer for drivers**

![Intel® Quartus® Prime Lite Edition Design Software](/get-started/browse-my-computer.png)

5. In the **Search for drivers in this location**, select **C:\intelFPGA_lite\18.1\quartus\drivers** and click next

![Intel® Quartus® Prime Lite Edition Design Software](/get-started/drivers.png)

6. Once it is successfully installed, the driver should be reflected in your Programmer

![Intel® Quartus® Prime Lite Edition Design Software](/get-started/altera-usb-blaster.png)

![Intel® Quartus® Prime Lite Edition Design Software](/get-started/hardware-found.png)
